FAQ

Common questions

How much can I realistically earn?
+
Our mentors earn between ₹5,000 and ₹38,000 per month depending on how many sessions they take. With 8 sessions/week at ₹399 each, you'd earn ~₹10,200/month. Most mentors spend only 4–6 hours per week on sessions.
How does verification work?
+
After you apply, we send a verification link to your official university email (e.g. @mit.edu or @ox.ac.uk). Clicking it confirms you're a genuine student. Your profile gets a "Verified" badge which students trust more — verified mentors get 3× more bookings.
When and how do I get paid?
+
You receive 80% of every session fee. Payments are processed within 3 working days of session completion — directly to your UPI ID or Indian bank account. No paperwork, no delays.
What if I need to cancel a session?
+
You can cancel any session with at least 24 hours notice with no penalty. Cancellations within 24 hours affect your response rate score. Repeated last-minute cancellations may affect your profile ranking — but life happens and we understand emergencies.
Do I need to be an Indian student?
+
Not at all! While our primary audience is Indian students applying abroad, any student enrolled at a recognized university can become a mentor on InsideUni. We welcome international mentors too.
Is InsideUni free to join as a mentor?
+
Completely free. There are no signup fees, no monthly charges, and no hidden costs. InsideUni takes a 20% platform commission only when you complete a session. If you don't earn, we don't earn.
